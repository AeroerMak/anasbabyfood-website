Our Story

Why Choose Anas?

Hey there, Superparents! Say hello to ANAS where a journey of love turned into Supermeals for your Superbabies!

We have cracked the code to make our foods friendly and accessible whenever you are! Your kiddos would be surprised that they will be diving into a world of Supermeals for lunch at the beach! Yep, we're sneaky like that!

We have packed enthusiasm at every step of our kitchen dance using a retort machine to bring you something truly extraordinary. From handpicking top-notch ingredients to creating Oriental and Malaysian flavours that will make your little one do a happy dance!

Our mission? To make your parenting journey a tad bit easier with wholesome meals, snacks from their first nibble to their 'I'm a big kid now' moments, we have got your back.
